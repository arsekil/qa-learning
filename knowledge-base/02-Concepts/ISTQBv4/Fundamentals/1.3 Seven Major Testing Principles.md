There are several observed testing principles for overall testing, seven of which are most effective and important to remember and understand.

1. Testing shows the presence, not the absence of defects
2. Exhaustive testing is impossible
3. Early testing saves time and money
4. Defects cluster together
5. Tests wear out
6. Testing is context dependent
7. Absence-of-defects fallacy

Plus Pareto principle: 80/20 rule - 20% smart work finds 80% bugs and defects
