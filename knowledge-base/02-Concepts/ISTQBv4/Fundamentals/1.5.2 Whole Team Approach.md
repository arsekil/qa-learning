**K1**

>[!important] Main Skill
>Ability to work effectively in team context and to contribute positively to team goals


The Whole Team Approach builds upon the above skill.

>[!important] Everyone is responsible for Quality

>[!important] Improves team dynamics

>[!important] Enhances communication and collabotation

>[!important] Creates synergy by allowing various skill sets to be leveraged for the benefit of the project

Testers work closely with other team members to ensure the desired quality levels. This includes collaborating with business representatives to create suitable acceptance tests ad working with developers to agree on test strategy and decide test automation approaches. Thus testers can influence the SDLC of the product.

>[!tip] Whole Team Approach Context Dependency
>It's not always appropriate, some situations need high level of test independence
