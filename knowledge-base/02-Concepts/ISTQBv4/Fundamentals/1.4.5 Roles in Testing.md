**K2**

1. Test Management Role
2. Testing Role

Activities and tasks assigned to these role depend on:
1. project
2. product context
3. skills of people in roles
4. organization

Test Management Role responsible for:
1. test process, test team, leadership of test activities
2. focuses on test planning, test monitoring, test control, test completion

Testing Role responsible for:
1. engineering (technical) aspect of testing
2. focuses on test analysis, test design, test implementation, test execution

Different people may take on these roles at different times e.g. team leader, test manager, developer manager. E.g. One person can take on both roles at the same time.
